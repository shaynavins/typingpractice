ABOUT TIPITYTYPE

With TipityType you can practice typing at any time. All you have to do is visit the page and start by visiting all the levels. As you begin, there will be tutorials that will guide you through many lessons. Each level holds its own stopwatch to track your time. As you complete every level, your time will be recorded. To view it, you can access the timings page by using the top navigation bar.
